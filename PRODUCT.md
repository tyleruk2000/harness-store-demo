# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Climbers shopping for gear — spanning newer gym climbers to experienced outdoor
and trad climbers. They arrive curious and often aspirational: browsing,
comparing, and buying harnesses, ropes, carabiners, shoes, and protection.
Context is pre-purchase research and discovery, on both mobile (on the go, at
the crag, between sessions) and desktop. The job to be done: find the right gear
quickly, trust that it's the real thing, and feel the pull to buy.

## Product Purpose

A storefront for climbing gear, built as a demo/showcase. It exists to prove
that an e-commerce experience can carry real personality and craft — the energy
of climbing itself — without falling into template sameness. Success looks like a
storefront a climber would screenshot and share: distinctly authored, fast,
trustworthy, and unmistakably *not* a stock theme.

## Brand Personality

Playful and energetic, with the underlying confidence that life-safety gear
demands. Voice: enthusiastic, plainspoken, fluent in real climbing culture — it
talks like someone who actually climbs, not a marketing department. Three words:
**energetic, playful, capable**. Emotional goals: excitement and momentum on
arrival; confidence and trust at the point of decision.

## Anti-references

- Generic Shopify / cookie-cutter e-commerce themes — the default storefront look.
- Identical icon-card grids and the SaaS hero-metric template.
- Sterile big-box outdoor-retailer sites (rows of products, no point of view).
- "Playful" mistaken for childish or cluttered — energy must stay sharp, not noisy.

## Design Principles

- **Energy with trust.** Playful and kinetic, but this is life-safety equipment;
  nothing in the design may undercut confidence in the gear or the purchase.
- **Movement is the metaphor.** Climbing is motion. Let layout, rhythm, and
  motion express upward momentum and dynamism rather than static rows.
- **Show the gear in its element.** Product over chrome — texture, real scale,
  real context. The gear and the climbing carry the page.
- **Earn every section.** Reject template defaults; each section should read as
  authored, not assembled. If it looks like a stock theme, rebuild it.
- **Fast and tactile.** Energetic never means slow. Snappy, responsive,
  hands-on interactions that reward touch.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA as the baseline: body text ≥ 4.5:1, large text ≥ 3:1.
- Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` — every energetic animation needs a calm
  crossfade or instant alternative.
- Full keyboard navigation across browse, product, and checkout flows.
- Never encode state (stock, selection, alerts) in color alone — pair with
  text, icon, or shape for color-blind users.
